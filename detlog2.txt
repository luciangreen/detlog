Detlog qa

1. Project scope
Should the first version be:
a Detlog-to-Prolog source converter,
a Detlog interpreter,
a Detlog-to-C compiler,
a Detlog-to-6502 compiler,

No, just basic Detlog.

3. Splice semantics

Yes.

A strict compiler rule
